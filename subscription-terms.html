Subscription Terms

Last Updated: May 12, 2026

1. Auto-Renewing Subscriptions

Image Resizer Pro offers Monthly and Annual subscription plans. By subscribing, you agree that your subscription will automatically renew at the end of each billing cycle.

  • Payment will be charged to your Google Play account at confirmation of purchase.
  • Auto-renew pricing will match the stated price unless notified in advance.
  • Account will be charged for renewal within 24-hours prior to the end of the current period.

2. Managing and Canceling

You have full control over your subscription through your Google Play account.

  • You can toggle auto-renewal off at any time from your Google Play Account Subscriptions panel.
  • Cancellations apply to the next billing cycle. You will retain Pro access for the remainder of your current paid period.

3. Lifetime Pro

If you purchase Lifetime Pro, it is a single, one-time transaction with no recurring fees, granting you permanent access to all Pro features.
