Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 12, 2026

1. Introduction

Image Resizer Pro ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

Key Principle: Your images never leave your device. All processing is done locally to ensure maximum privacy and security.

2. Information We Collect

We believe in data minimization. Here is a breakdown of what we do and do not collect:

  • Personal Data: We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or phone number.
  • Image Data: We do not upload your images to any servers. All resizing, cropping, and compression happen entirely on your device.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics (e.g., which features are used most) to improve the app. This data cannot be traced back to you.

3. Permissions

To function correctly, the App requires certain permissions:

  • Storage/Media Access: Needed to read the images you want to resize and save the processed results back to your gallery.
  • Internet Access: Used for showing advertisements (in the free version) and processing subscription/pro feature verification.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google Play Services: For app functionality and updates.
  • AdMob: For displaying advertisements. You can manage your ad preferences in your Google Account settings.
  • Firebase Analytics: For anonymous crash reporting and usage analysis.

5. Children's Privacy

These Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
