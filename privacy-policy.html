Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 12, 2026

1. Introduction

Image Resizer Pro ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

Key Principle: Your images never leave your device. All processing is done locally to ensure maximum privacy and security.

2. Information We Collect

We believe in strict data minimization. Here is a breakdown of what we do and do not collect:

  • Personal Data: We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or phone number.
  • Image Data & Content: We never upload your photos or images to our servers. All image processing—including resizing, cropping, format conversion, and compression—happens 100% locally on your device. We do not have access to, nor do we store, any of the images you process within the app.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous, aggregated usage statistics (e.g., which features or tools are used most frequently) to help us improve the app experience. This data cannot be traced back to you as an individual.

3. Permissions & Background Activity

To provide a seamless experience, the App requires the following permissions:

  • Storage/Media Access: Needed to read the images you want to resize and save the processed results back to your gallery.
  • Notifications: We request notification access to keep you informed about the progress of your image processing tasks and to alert you when a batch operation is finished.
  • Background Processing: For large batch operations, the App may request to run in the background. This ensures that your work is not interrupted if you switch to another application or lock your screen.
  • Internet Access: Used for advertisements (in the free version) and processing subscription/pro feature verification.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses trusted third-party services that may collect information used to identify you or process your transactions:

  • Google Play Services: Used for core app functionality and distributing updates.
  • RevenueCat: Used to manage and validate your premium subscriptions and in-app purchases securely. RevenueCat processes anonymous App User IDs and purchase receipts, but never has access to your personal images.
  • AdMob: Used for displaying advertisements in the free version. You can manage your personalized ad preferences directly in your Google Account settings.
  • Firebase Analytics: Used for anonymous crash reporting and usage analysis to help us fix bugs quickly.

5. Children's Privacy

Our Services do not address anyone under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from children under 13.

If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will immediately delete such data. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information or made unauthorized in-app purchases, please contact us immediately at jasu1070@gmail.com so we can take the necessary actions.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
